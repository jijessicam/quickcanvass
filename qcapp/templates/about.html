Campaign Managers

Frequently Asked Questions: Campaign Managers

{% if isd == 1 %}

  • How do I add volunteers to my campaign?

    Give them your 8 digit campaign code (see the number underneath the title-about of your campaign in your Manager Dashboard), and tell them to enter it on their Volunteer for Campaigns page.

  • How do I make minor changes to my survey?

    On your Manager Dashboard page, click the blue "Edit Current Survey" button.

  • How do I make major changes to my survey?

    On your Manager Dashboard page, click the blue "Start a New Survey" button. This will download your data, clear survey responses, and allow you to choose new survey questions.

  • How do I tell if my change is minor or major?

    If it's a typo or changing the script, it's minor. If you're fundamentally changing a survey question, it's probably major. A major change is one that would require canvassing students again.

  • How do I download my survey data?

    On your Manager Dashboard page, click the blue "Download Survey Data" button.

  • Can I volunteer for my own campaign?

    Yep - check out the "Campaign Volunteers" section of this page.

Campaign Volunteers

Frequently Asked Questions: Campaign Volunteers

  • How do I join a campaign?

    Ask the person in charge of the campaign for your 8-digit campaign code, and then enter that where it tells you to "type in your campaign key" on your Volunteer Dashboard.

  • How do I see what campaigns I'm a volunteer for?

    Look at the list under "Your Current Campaigns" on your Volunteer Dashboard.

  • How do I canvass for my campaign?

    Click on the campaign you want to canvass for on your Volunteer Dashboard. Arrange the search paramaters to your desire, and then click "Find Students!". This will show you the students who meet the critera you've selected.
    To record data on any of these students, click the "Canvass" button on the right hand side of the table. This will show you a screen with the script made by the owner of the campaign, as well as any questions that the owner wanted you to ask the student. Once you've talked to the student, record their answers and then hit Submit (or Cancel if they weren't home).

  • I haven't searched for anything yet but there are students listed on the Canvass It page?

    For your convenience, the table will automatically be populated according to your last search - feel free to search again though.

  • Which students will be shown when I click the "Find Students!" button?

    Undergraduate students meeting the criteria you've selected who have not been canvassed yet by your campaign.

  • Should I be signed up as a manager or a volunteer?

    If you want to be able to direct the campaign or organize volunteers, sign up as a manager. Usually, this will be either the candidate or the actual campaign manager for the candidate. If you're here to knock on some doors for your friend, you're a volunteer. Managers are able to join other campaigns and knock on doors for their campaign.

  • How can I become a manager and create a campaign if I signed up as a volunteer initially?

    Click "Initiate a New Campaign" and get started!
